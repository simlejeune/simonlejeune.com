Simon is the Chief Growth Officer at Wealthsimple ($10B, fintech). Belgian-born entrepreneur in Montreal, he led growth at the two highest-valued consumer tech companies in Canada — Wealthsimple and Hopper ($5B, travel app). Built and sold a growth agency (acquired by Wealthsimple). Advises venture-backed startups in fintech, crypto and AI.

From 2019 to 2025, Simon led digital strategy for Les Engagés, a Belgian political party that he helped double its vote share from ~11% to ~21% and return to all levels of government.
